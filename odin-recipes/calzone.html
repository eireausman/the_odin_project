Calzone

Folding it up with some cheesey mix.

Pineapple you say? In a Calzone? It's coming right up.

We take our pizzas seriously and that's why we serve our calzone with a legally wordy mouth-burn non-liability clause for you to sign at ouu leisure.

Ingredients:
  • Hot hot oil
  • Cheeses (all of them)
  • Half a pineapple
  • Half a pineapple (again)
  • Some salty meats
  • Cherry tomatoes, not sliced so they become 'hot bombs'
Method Steps:
  1. They key to any good pizza dough is a moustache and a black and white striped jumper. Got get 'em on.
  2. With all of your artistic flare, roll some dough and spin that bad boy around the dance floor for at least 20 mins.
  3. Make a big circle (out of the dough!) and then throw on all the ingredients
  4. Make a big pasty (ahem, pizza) by folding the circle of doubh and crimping the edges
  5. Get the wood and the pizza oven ready. And the pizza paddle.
  6. Cook for maximum for of 7 seconds to avoid neighbourhood meltdown. Serve (with oven set to OFF).
